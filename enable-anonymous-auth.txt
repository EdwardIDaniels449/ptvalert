# 如何在Firebase控制台中启用匿名登录

按照以下步骤启用Firebase项目中的匿名登录功能:

## 中文版说明

1. 访问 [Firebase控制台](https://console.firebase.google.com/)
2. 选择您的项目 "ptvalert-19ea4"
3. 在左侧导航栏中，点击 "Authentication"
4. 点击 "Sign-in method" 标签页
5. 在认证提供方列表中找到 "Anonymous" (匿名登录)
6. 点击 "Anonymous" 行右侧的编辑图标(铅笔图标)
7. 将匿名登录方式的开关切换为开启状态
8. 点击 "保存" 按钮

启用后，您的应用将能够使用匿名登录功能。

## English Instructions

1. Visit the [Firebase Console](https://console.firebase.google.com/)
2. Select your project "ptvalert-19ea4"
3. In the left navigation bar, click "Authentication"
4. Click on the "Sign-in method" tab
5. Find "Anonymous" in the list of authentication providers
6. Click the edit icon (pencil icon) on the right side of the "Anonymous" row
7. Toggle the switch for anonymous authentication to the enabled state
8. Click the "Save" button

After enabling, your application will be able to use anonymous authentication.

注意/Note:
如果您在网页控制台看到 "auth/configuration-not-found" 或 "auth/operation-not-allowed" 错误，
这通常表示您需要在Firebase控制台中启用匿名登录功能。

If you see "auth/configuration-not-found" or "auth/operation-not-allowed" errors in your web console,
this typically indicates you need to enable Anonymous sign-in in the Firebase console.
